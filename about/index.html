About Me

My name is Oscar Romarchelino and I am a student at Gadjah Mada University (UGM), where I am currently pursuing a degree in Philosophy. My studies interest focus on the works of Deleuze and Guattari, Zizek, Nietzsche, Betrand Russel, Wittgenstein, and among others. In my free time, I indulge in my passion for coding, with a particular interest in the programming languages Lua, Python, and Rust. I have also dabbled in web development, with a focus on front-end technologies.

I also created a website to share my thoughts, ideas and projects with the world. I have a keen interest in technology and use it to improve people's lives. I hope that my website serves as an inspiration to others who share the same passion. In my website, you will find a wide range of topics, from programming and design to personal development and more. When I need a break from studying and coding, I love to unwind by playing my favorite games, Minecraft and Growtopia. These diverse interests and activities keep my mind constantly engaged and stimulated.
